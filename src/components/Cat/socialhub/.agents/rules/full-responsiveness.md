---
description: Full Responsiveness
---

Always build mobile-first and ensure full responsiveness from 360px to 2560px+.

Requirements:
- Preserve existing design, spacing, typography, and animations.
- Do not change paddings, margins, gaps, or sizes unless required for responsiveness.
- Prevent horizontal scrolling and overflow.
- Use Flexbox, CSS Grid, clamp(), minmax(), %, rem, vw, and vh units where appropriate.
- Ensure layouts work on 360px, 480px, 768px, 1024px, 1440px, 1920px, and 2560px screens.
- Images, cards, forms, and sections must scale proportionally.
- Maintain agency-level visual quality across all screen sizes.
- Keep code clean, performant, and accessible.
